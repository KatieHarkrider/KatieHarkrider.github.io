I am currently a Social Media Manager and Communications Specialist, with experience in event planning and project management.

Social Media Manager

I have complete ownership of 8 Facebook Pages, 4 Twitter accounts, 3 Instagram profiles, and 4 YouTube channels. I curate content and copy for each page weekly and keep a monthly list of all holidays, national days or events going on within the company. I track analytics for every post on each platform and keep track of monthly follower counts. Communications Specialist

I planned and coordinated all virtual events and virtual speakers after March 2020. Work extensively in Adobe, Microsoft Office, and have experience working with Mailchimp for sending out press releases and Meltwater for maintaining contacts and press lists.
